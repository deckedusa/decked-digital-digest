
# Accessory Orders - Auto Generate Fulfillments

We refactored accessory auto fulfillment to utilize a Map/Reduce script that runs every 15 mins to look for accessory orders that are ready to fulfill. It updates Dispatch Status to YES and then calls a Suitelet to generate the fulfillment.

- Map / Reduce -[SG2x_MR_Create_Item_Fulfillments](https://4532811.app.netsuite.com/app/common/scripting/script.nl?id=4960&id=4960&whence=)
